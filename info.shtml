---传播智慧和爱，让每个人都可以为自己的真实梦想发声。作为新梦想大会的创办人，李孟桥的这份理想已经践行了两年。从英国留学归来之后，她一边从事着学业所涉的影视文化，另一边收集古董珠宝，用心呵护它们的同时，她还在太庙定期举办公益活动，宣传健康的素食主义澄净梦想。

--- Pass on wisdom and love; Let everyone speak for their true dreams. As the founder of True Dream Conference, Queena has followed her motto for two years. Since returning to China after the study in UK, she has been working in cinema and TV industry as she is trained, collecting and cherishing antique jewelries, and holding philanthropic events in Imperial Ancestral Temple to promote vegan and cleansing life style.

“完成梦想需要智慧,但更需要爱,而这种爱类似慈悲,它关注的是所有人,所有万物生灵。梦想就像种子,它会跟随我们的发心,一起成长。不论追梦的过程中遇到怎样的艰难和阻挠。我们都无需慌张也不用沮丧,只要时时内观我们的发心,是纯粹的,没有改变,那梦想一定会在因缘具足的时候成就结果。”

“To achieve a dream, you need wisdom. But you need love more. The type of love needed is similar to benevolence which takes a focus on everyone and all the living. Dream is like a seed. It grows in the same pace of our motivation. No matter what obstacles and difficulties we encounter while chasing our dreams, we need not to panic neither should we get frustrated. What we should do is to introspect on our motivation: if it has remained the same and pure, then our dream will come true when it is the time.”

作为新梦想大会的创办人，李孟桥已经不只一次在演讲中介绍素食主义。言语有时会发声化学反应。当许多人问起她举办新梦想大会是否就是为了推崇素食？她的“微笑”很少有人懂。

As the founder of True Dream Conference, Queena has more than once mentioned vegetarianism in her speeches and roused curiosity over whether her True Dream Conference was only established for promotion of vegetarianism. In reply she smiled and few people understood her.

李孟桥在英国学习电影与传媒，至今投入到影视里的时间和投身在公益事业的时间不分伯仲。在没有新梦想大会之前，她也将公益宣传，尤其是环境、动物保护放在要点位置。

Queena studied cinema and media in UK. She has invested equal time into cinema and her philanthropic activities. Before founding the conference, she has also been advocating environment protection and animal protection.

“素食是一个值得介绍的选择”所以，她将全世界最古老的素食组织引入到中国，成立了The Chinese Vegan Society。VEGAN音译为维根，它的定义是：一种不剥削和虐待动物、不以动物为食、不穿动物做的衣服以及不用动物另作它途的生活方式。虽然有很多人称它为“纯素食者”。但其实不仅仅是关乎食物。2014年的新梦想大会，主题是“未来是什么？”，来自全球不同领域的精英领袖都对“未来”表达了他们不同的见解和期望。电动方程式赛车FormulaE的Alejandro Agag 讲述了《未来是电动的》；美国责任医师协会大中华区总干事徐嘉博士讲述了《未来的饮食和医疗》；菩提本愿会会长、慈爱基金的发起人加措活佛讲述了《云端上的爱》；PETA 善待动物组织副总裁JASON BAKER 讲述了《未来就是现在》；电讯盈科有限公司总裁 程十庆 讲述了 《心有所寄 诗意生活》；卢森堡大公国驻华总领事 Luc Decker讲述了《国家点亮未来的希望》。新梦想创始人李孟桥也在新梦想大会那天的太庙现场发言，她说：“无论你来自哪里，从事任何行业，梦想是每个人都有的念头。它或大或小。而对于“新梦想”来说，智慧和爱超越一切。如果没有智慧，追求梦想的执着就会变成一种痛苦。即使是在坚守一个善举，如果没有智慧，这种善举也会膨胀自我。我们建议大家选择零污染，零伤害，零负担的电动车、选择简单的素食和心有所寄的诗意生活。但我仍然想说，希望自己拥有一颗了解智慧和慈悲的心，是更为珍贵的新梦想。“

“Vegetarian life style is a worthy choice.” She introduced world’s oldest vegetarian organization into China and set up The Chinese Vegan Society. Vegan is defined as a life style without exploiting, abusing, eating and wearing animals, neither using animals for other purposes. A lot of people call it absolute vegetarian. But it is more than just about food. The 2014 conference is featured What Is Future. Elites from all backgrounds and all parts of the world expressed their expectation for future. Alejandro Agag from FormulaE made a speech titled Future is Electrified; Dr. Xu Jia, Secretary General ofPhysicians Committee for Responsible Medicine Greater China made a speech on Future Dining and Healthcare; Jiacuo Living Budda, Head of Bodhi Will Society and Founder of Benevolence Fund talked about Love on the Clouds; Jason Baker, Vice President of PETA animal welfare organization made a speech titled “Future is Now”; Cheng Shiqing, President of Pacific Century Cyber Works made a speech titled “Poetic Life with Spiritual Sustenance”; and Luc Decker, Consulate General to China of Luxembourg made a speech titled Nations to Light Up the Hope for Future”. Queena also spoke at the conference, “No matter where you are from, what you are doing, you have a dream. It can be big and can be small. For True Dream, wisdom and love transcend all. Without wisdom, persistence in your dream will be painful. Even gestures out of kindness will become about one’s ego without support of wisdom. We advise for zero pollution, zero harm and zero burden electrical bikes; for simple vegetarian food and life with spiritual sustenance. I would also like to say that I hope I have a heart to understand wisdom and kindness for the sake of true dreams.”

就像最初创办新梦想大会的时候，她希望给每个人不一样的发声平台，所以当企业家在讲述创业梦想和社会责任的时候，落座其中的旁观者都觉得这是奇招的时候，李孟桥的所思所想却只是单纯地要搭建一个自由、包容的平台，在这个平台上分享的智慧思想，也许不是谋生的方式，也许不能促进经济发展和物质建设，也许在很多人眼中近乎“无用”，却能够在潜移默化中改变很多人和事。

Like when she started True Dream, she envisioned it as a platform of speaking up for everyone. When entrepreneurs talk about dreams and social responsibilities, the audience all find the solution excellent. For Queena, her purpose remained the same – to set up a free and inclusive platform for wisdom sharing. This platform won’t teach how to survive or contribute to economic development. But the “useless” subjects are influencing and changing people and things.

正因为如此，新梦想大会第二届刚落下帷幕，第三届已经在筹备期。在北京的秋天刚刚来到的时候，李孟桥又将告别中国近两个月的时间。这次，她的目的地是印度。一方面，她将开始短片摄制，另一方面，她在为理想中的新梦想而筹备。下一站的大会愿将世界上珍贵却不一定人尽皆知的智慧学府介绍给大家，就教育问题展开新一轮的梦想探讨。

Therefore right after the close of the second conference, Queena is preparing for the third conference. Early autumn arrived in Beijing and Queena would leave China for close to two months. Her destination is India. On one hand, she will produce a show; on the other hand, she is preparing for her ideal True Dream. In the next conference, wisdom of value but not known to all will be shared and education will be discussed.

比如印度那烂陀大学… 那个著名的十公里长，五公里宽的学府，玄奘曾在那里求学。时隔八百年之后，那烂陀大学再度复课，它的校长是诺贝尔经济学奖获得者。用佛陀的智慧，解决现代社会的问题，在李孟桥看来，这是一件很有价值推广的事情，也是一个值得介绍的选择。她将信息告诉了身边的朋友，也有基金会、教育机构的朋友听到信息后给予积极相应的反馈。新梦想是一个没有指定任务的平台，但在这个过程中因缘际会地结识一些人一些事情，所以很多事情都是萌发于发心和因缘。

For example, Nalanda University, in a 10km*5km building where Xuanzang studied, was brought back to life and headed by a winner of Nobel Economic Prize. For Queena, using Buddha’s wisdom to solve modern society problems is a philosophy worth promoting and a worthy choice. She told this to her friends and people in funds and educational organizations. All gave her positive feedback. True Dream is a platform without any tasks. People meet people at the conference and this is all from the heart and is part of fate.

再追问到往后的规划，李孟桥说：能做多少做多少，没那么在意结果。

When asked about future plans, Queena said, “I’ll do as much as I can. I’m not worried about what I achieve.”

“我的想法其实很简单，就是希望搭建一个让大家畅所欲言的平台。那些平日没有机会分享的见解和主张，可能不够主流，可能异想天开，可能很多被现代社会认为“无用”，然而其实却充满智慧和慈悲！为了支持相同的理念，组委会很多参与者都是没有酬劳地在做这件事情……”李孟桥自己就是典型的例子。她是最大的为梦想买单的人，而在她的感召下，许多人都不计酬劳甚至自掏腰包地参与进来。

“My thoughts are very simple. I hope to establish a platform where people can say whatever they want to say. Some ideas may not be main stream and even illusive so useless under normal social rules. But these ideas can be very wise and kind. To support the same ideas, the organizing committee is doing everything for free.” Queena is the best example. She is the one who paid the bill and under her influence, many have also pulled money out of their own pocket and joined the mission.

不那么在意结果，但过程中始终怀有梦想，就像她选择做的每一件事，都是希望尽一己微小之力，播撒正念的种子。

Not caring too much about achievement but keeping dream in mind is what she brings in when she does everything – giving away positive seeds with all she has.
